About Us!

What started as a home-based business, hand-calligrapher, Kathryn Podorsky and long-time friend of over 15 years, Alisa Kennedy, have come together to begin a thriving new business. As Kathryn's calligraphy business grew, she solicited the help of Alisa for various jobs. Alisa has aided in areas including bookkeeping, font designing, assembly and packaging of products, proofreading, and various creative design work. With over 10 years of retail and management experience, Alisa has a keen eye for detail and is talented in spotting trend-setting merchandise.
